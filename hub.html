Learn how to be Green and track your good actions!

Seattle Big Green Challenge is a new competitive social game by Seattle Public Utilities and Seattle City Light.

Being green is easy and fun. With Big Green Challenge you can learn how to be eco-friendly, earn achievements, and share what you're learning and doing with your friends!
Here's how it works:

  1. Take a step towards being green. We have a wide range of recommendations, from trying about one of SPU's helpful tools to learning about green landscaping to changing the way you do laundry.
  2. Claim the relevant badge here. Over time you'll gain points and badges, which unlock other awards.
  3. Share your progress via Facebook or Twitter to compete with your friends and share what you've learned.

Awards

There are four types of awards you can earn with Seattle Green Achievers:

  • Badges represent individual, distinct green actions. Badges can be claimed multiple times if you repeat the green action; keeping track will help you make progress towards earning ribbons.
  • Trophies measure your willingness to experiment and the breadth of your green actions. Earning distinct badges in a category will get you closer to earning trophies.
  • Ribbons reward consistency and building environmentally friendly habits. They are earned for your total number of green actions in a given category, including multiple claims of the same badge.
  • Stickers are secret awards for unique combinations of badges. Earn as many different badges as possible to discover new stickers!

All four types of awards will appear in your case once earned. Progress towards trophies and ribbons can be tracked here.

Technical Details and Saving Your Progress

Seattle Big Green Challenge uses Javascript and HTML5 Local Storage. If either is disabled, you won't be able to use this site. Seattle Big Green Challenge is supported by modern browsers; if you're having trouble, consider updating your browser.

Saving Your Progress

Seattle Big Green Challenge automatically saves your progress using local storage. However, clearing your browser cache can delete local storage data. To make sure that you don't lose your progress, you can save the text below. Enter the text in the Load Data field and submit to restore your progress. Altering the text may compromise your progress and the function of this site.
