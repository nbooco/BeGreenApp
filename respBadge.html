Learn how to be Green and track your good actions!

Seattle Green Achievers is a new competitive social game by Seattle Public Utilities and Seattle City Light.

Being green is easy and fun. With Green Achievers you can learn how to be eco-friendly, earn achievements, and share what you're learning and doing with your friends!
Here's how it works:

  1. Take a step towards being green. We have a wide range of recommendations, from trying about one of SPU's helpful tools to learning about green landscaping to changing the way you do laundry.
  2. Claim the relevant badge here. Over time you'll gain points and badges, which unlock other awards.
  3. Share your progress via Facebook or Twitter to compete with your friends and share what you've learned.
